AUDIO-BASED NEWS PRODUCED FOR COMMUNITIES BY COMMUNITIES

The Rise is an audio-based information platform that provides opportunities for individuals to come together and discuss pressing matters within their communities. We are on a mission to mobilize change in the real world by elevating high-quality information and bringing together well-informed people.

Through designing an environment that respects human nature and gives everyone autonomy over the material they produce and engage with, niche topics are uncovered that typically wouldn't be found in mainstream media. We chose the medium of a website over an app in order to enforce a sit-down experience, this way individuals are fully engaged and prepared to participate in intellectual discussions; the constant distraction and multitasking that smartphones enable can affect the overall quality of information being developed.

THE IMPACTS OF INHUMANE TECHNOLOGY

The digital social space is damaging human behaviour and failing to positively impact our real world existence. This is due to it being riddled with low-quality information, misinformation, and polarization; whilst also fuelling pseudo-connections and superficial interactions. Ad-based business models exploit our innate physiological vulnerabilities and profit off addictive behavioural patterns. Certain information is amplified for the purpose of keeping “users” engaged. The effects of this are damaging cognitive development along with physical and mental health. The lack of exposure people are having to diverse perspectives and the truth is leading to systemic oppression, a disrupted democratic process and reduced empathy.
