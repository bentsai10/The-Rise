AUDIO-BASED NEWS PRODUCED FOR COMMUNITIES BY COMMUNITIES

The Rise is on a mission to make information more democratized, truthful, valuable and diverse. We elevate knowledge that provides significant value to tight-knit communities and one's existence in the physical world.

Through designing an environment that respects human nature and gives everyone autonomy over the content they produce and engage with, niche topics are uncovered that typically wouldn't be found in mainstream media; it also makes for a far more productive, enjoyable and healthy online experience.

THE IMPACTS OF INHUMANE TECHNOLOGY

HUMAN BEHAVIOR

The digital social space is riddled with low-quality information, misinformation, and polarization. People lack significant exposure to diverse perspectives and the truth; this is leading to systemic oppression, a disrupted democratic process and reduced empathy.

Learn More

HUMAN WELL-BEING

Both social media channels and mainstream news outlets adopt ad-based business models which exploit our innate physiological vulnerabilities and profit off addictive behavioral patterns. Certain information is amplified for the purpose of keeping “users” engaged. The effects of this are damaging cognitive development along with physical and mental health.
